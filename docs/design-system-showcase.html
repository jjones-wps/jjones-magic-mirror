Design Principles
01

Whisper, Don't Shout

Information appears gently, fades gracefully. The black background dominates; white is precious, used sparingly.

02

Breathing Rhythm

All animations follow organic, breath-like timing. Slow pulses create subconscious calm, not anxiety.

03

Intelligent Negative Space

60%+ of the screen is pure black. Information floats in darkness like stars. Space IS the design.

04

Motion as Meaning

Vertical motion = time flow. Horizontal = category. Fade = state change. Every animation communicates.
